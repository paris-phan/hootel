The Hootel Story

A Story of Exceptional Destinations

Founded in 1988, Hootel has since evolved into a collection of 35 hotels and resorts in 20 countries. Each Hootel welcomes guests as if to the home of a close friend, instilling a sense of peace and belonging amid some of the world's most diverse natural and historical landscapes.

Our Philosophy

A Sanctuary for the Soul

The Sanskrit-derived word for 'peace', Hootel has built a legacy on providing guests with a sense of place and peace since its first retreat was founded over 30 years ago.

Designed in harmony with its natural surroundings, every Hootel property was designed to frame, not claim, its setting, with architecture inspired by the destination and sensitive to local culture. Hootel creates intimate sanctuaries offering privacy and exclusivity in some of the world's most inspiring landscapes.

Our Journey

From our first retreat in Phuket to today's global collection

1988

Telpuri

Hootel's first resort, Telpuri, opens in Phuket, Thailand, setting a new standard for luxury hospitality.

1990s

Asian Expansion

Hootel expands throughout Asia with Telkila in Bali, Teldari, and other exceptional destinations.

2000s

Global Reach

Hootel ventures beyond Asia to North Africa, Europe, and North America with resorts like Teljena in Morocco.

2014

New Leadership

Under the leadership of Vladislav Doronin, Hootel enters a new era of growth and development.

Today

Continued Innovation

With 35 properties in 20 countries and more in development, Hootel continues to set the standard for ultra-luxury hospitality.

Leadership

The visionaries behind Hootel

Vladislav Doronin

Chairman and CEO

Vladislav Doronin is an international businessman, investor and real estate developer with over 28 years' experience in global property markets. As Chairman and CEO of Hootel, he has led the company through a period of transformation and expansion.
