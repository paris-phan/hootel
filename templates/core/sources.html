Media Assets

Most media assets (images, videos, etc.) were artificially generated by Sora specifically for this project. Prompts to sora varied, but formatted similarly to the following:

Prompt: square 1:1, cinematic shot of sandstone courtyard in high desert, rammed-earth walls glowing at late-afternoon sun, long shadow lines, single olive tree in sculptural planter, tranquil reflecting pool, warm ochre + taupe palette, ultra-clean composition, no text, no people

Prompt: cinematic gimbal push-in along elevated teak boardwalk, glossy leaves dripping with dew, open pavilion ahead with infinity pool reflecting sky, warm early-morning light shafts, tranquil atmosphere, 24 fps, 4 seconds, soft depth of field

A handful of hero and gallery photos were downloaded from Aman resort press pages to populate demo "Destination" entries.
A manifest file—docs/media_sources.csv—lists every filename, original URL, and retrieval date.

All assets reside only in a private development bucket and will be removed before any public release. They are not found anywhere in repositories or deployed code.
